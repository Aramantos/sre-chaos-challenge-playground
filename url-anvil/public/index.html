URL Anvil

A micro-benchmark service to test the performance of external URLs by fetching them concurrently and hashing their content.

How to Use for Challenges

  • Graceful Degradation Challenge: Use the URL Anvil to test the resilience of your service. By subjecting it to a high load, you can observe how your service handles stress and maintains availability.
  • Robust Service Challenge: Integrate the URL Anvil into your service's testing pipeline. The `total_fetch_time_ms` and `average_fetch_time_ms` metrics can help you evaluate the efficiency and responsiveness of your service under load.
  • Basic Usage: Enter URLs in the text area, click "Test URLs" to run a benchmark, or "Load Sample URLs" for a quick start.

Result:

Awaiting test...

Output Explanation:

  • urls_tested: The number of URLs successfully fetched and processed.
  • total_fetch_time_ms: The total time in milliseconds taken to fetch all URLs concurrently.
  • average_fetch_time_ms: The average time in milliseconds per URL fetch.
  • content_hash: A SHA-256 hash of the combined content from all fetched URLs. This verifies content integrity.
  • timestamp: The UTC timestamp when the test was completed.
